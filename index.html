Biography

I am an Control and Automation Engineer from Brazil. I received a MSc. degree in Automation and Systems Engineering from the Federal University of Santa Catarina (UFSC) in 2020.

My dissertation, defended in July 2020, focuses on Bilevel optimization, Benders Decomposition and Outer Approximation applied to a distributed control system (DMPC) with limited resource constraints. It was supervised by Eduardo Campnogara (UFSC), Laio O. Seman (UNIVALI) and Helton F. Scherer (Itaipu). During this project I used the Julia programming language together with the JuMP package to model and iterate with several solvers, being one of the first to use Julia in the department.

Before starting the masters, I worked in a couple of start-ups, from an IoT/Computer Vision startup to house automation company. And I did an MBA in Project Management at the Fundação Getulio Vargas.

On a personal note, I read both fiction and non-fiction books, on economic policy, strategy, and entrepreneurship. I also enjoy games in various formats (video, board, card) and cooking.
